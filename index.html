42 Porto – Programming

Here you can find some of the programming projects I have developed so far at 42 Porto, as well as other projects I got involved in along the way.

42 Porto is part of the global 42 Network, following a peer-to-peer, project-based learning model. This way, its programme encourages students to learn through collaboration, self-directed study, and hands-on projects.

Diálogo Fotográfico

Photography workshop aimed at senior students of Universidade Sénior de Ermesinde.

The territory of Gandra, in particular its connection with the railroad - broadly associated with the theme of “journeys” - was the motto for the workshop. The resulting photographs were displayed at EntreLinhas – railway festival.

De Zhejiang para o Porto

An exhibition narrating the migration journey of an individual (my grandfather, Chow Horng Tzer 周洪泽) and his family, and his contribute to the Chinese Migrants community in Portugal. The exhibition was held at the Manuel António da Mota Foundation to mark the first anniversary of his passing.

Four illustrations created for the first series of Doc's Kingdom online masterclasses exploring the idea of community. Todas as Coisas: Brotéria 1902—2020

Brotéria is a monthly magazine, published since 1902. Along with its vast library, it relocated to a historical building in Bairro Alto in early 2020. It was a new beginning for the publication, that opened to the city and its participants, inaugurating a cultural center. Todas as Coisas was the center's opening exhibition.
